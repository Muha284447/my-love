A Letter For You

I sealed this one properly. Open it whenever you're ready.

My love,

If you're reading this, it means you found the little world I built for you — pixel by pixel, the same way I've tried to build a life around loving you well.

I don't know how to compress everything I feel into words, but I'll try. You are the calm at the end of my longest days and the reason ordinary Tuesdays feel like something worth remembering. You make me want to be steadier, kinder, and braver than I already am — not because you ask me to, but because that's what loving you naturally pulls out of me.

I love the way you laugh at your own jokes before you even finish telling them. I love how seriously you take the people you care about, and how lucky I feel to be one of them. I love that being near you makes even quiet, uneventful moments feel like they matter.

So here is a small, imperfect website trying to say a very big, very simple thing: you are the most important person in my life, and I don't say that lightly. Thank you for choosing me, again and again, on the ordinary days as much as the extraordinary ones.

Yours, always.
